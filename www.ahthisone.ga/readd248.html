THE SELF TAUGHT PATH

They are many ONLINE RESOURCES to learn Computer Science.
But Most of the websites are either paid or don't provide upto the mark Quality or Insufficient some where here or there lacking something.

Just jumping on the INTERNET HERE AND THERE I HAVE CAME ACROSS SOME OF THE USEFUL RESOURCES.

1. https://teachyourselfcs.com/
: A organize resources list with some FAQ answered

2. https://github.com/ossu/computer-science#readme
: A well long organized list of Video courses list ~ path to be self taught at CS

3. https://github.com/dipakkr/A-to-Z-Resources-for-Students
: a List of Resources consist of various kind of stuffs forums and other links

4. https://www.scotthyoung.com/blog/myprojects/mit-challenge-2/
: A Project of learning CS in just 1 Year using MIT OCW resources

5. https://github.com/watson/awesome-computer-history
: consist of various Docs & Video on Computer.

6. http://www.infocobuild.com/ Site contain well arranged OCWs & other resources for learners

It will be awesome if you will explorer them yourself and get a VISION of it.
